Koraput Nari Sakti Farmers Producer Company Limited

Our roots

Koraput Nari Sakti Farmers Producer Company Limited (KNSFPCL) stands as a beacon of agricultural empowerment, embodying the spirit of progress and sustainability. Established on 23 July 2020 and headquartered in Cuttack, Odisha, KNSFPCL is a private company dedicated to uplifting farmers in the region. Classified as a Non-government company, it is registered with the Registrar of Companies, Cuttack. With a vision firmly rooted in the prosperity of its members, KNSFPCL boasts an authorized share capital of Rs. 2,500,000 and a paid-up capital of Rs. 2,154,600. The company's core focus lies in the cultivation of crops, market gardening, and horticulture, embodying a commitment to organic and sustainable farming practices. At its heart, KNSFPCL is driven by a mission to empower farmers, especially women, in the Koraput region of Odisha. By providing them with a platform to collaborate and grow, the company aims to enhance agricultural productivity and economic opportunities.

Our vision and mission


Vision

Koraput Nari Sakti Farmers Producer Company Limited (KNSFPCL) envisions a future where the agricultural landscape of Koraput, Odisha thrives with sustainable practices, empowered farmers, and safe, high-quality produce. Our vision is to be a beacon of eco-sustainability, enabling farmers to cultivate low volume – high-value organically produced crops.

Mission

Our mission at KNSFPCL is deeply rooted in serving our customers with top-notch, quality products, grown and nurtured with care. We are committed to being farmer-friendly, ensuring that our farmers receive adequate returns for their hard work and dedication. Every step we take is with the safety of our consumers in mind, offering produce that is not only delicious but also safe for consumption. At KNSFPCL, sustainability is not just a goal but a way of life, as we work towards creating a planet-friendly farming model that benefits our environment and future generations.
