Our roots

The Indian Organic Farmers Producer Company Limited (IOFPCL), headquartered in Aluva, Kerala is the largest organic producer company in India owned by the farmers. We serve more than 2500 primary producer members in Kerala, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu.

IOFPCL was founded in the year 2004 and incorporated under the Companies Act of 1956 is emboldened to take on the challenges to assist the member farmers in the production and marketing of Organic and Fairtrade certified products in the domestic and international markets. At the end of 2004, the Indian Organic Farmers Producers Company, formed a small group of farmers from the districts like Wayanad, Kannur, Palakkad and Idukki in Kerala. They were trained in organic farming under the leadership of the company and practiced organic farming.
