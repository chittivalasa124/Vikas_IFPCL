FACTORIES

We produce best quality chocolates, spices, coffee and coconut products.

Wayanad

Coffee

We procure the best quality Robusta Coffee from our member farmers in the Wayanad region. Our coffee is shade-grown at an elevation of 600 - 900 meters above mean sea level. A large variety of crops like pepper, cardamom, vanilla, ginger, and banana grow alongside coffee plants. Our coffee is mild with a fine aroma.

Spices

A wide variety of spices are procured from the Wayanad and Idukki districts of Kerala, which is considered home to several spices. The area has always reigned supreme in the production and export of the most exotic and sight-after spices. Our range of spices includes Black and White Pepper, Nutmeg, Vanilla, Ginger, Turmeric, Clove, Cinnamon, and Cardamom.

Palakkad

Coconut products

Kerala is famous for its coconut plantations. Organic coconut, coconut oil, virgin coconut oil, and desiccated coconut have a growing demand in the world market today. We procure organic coconut from our member farmers in the Palakkad and Kannur districts of Kerala.

Kottayam

Cocoa products

High-quality cocoa is obtained from our member farmers in the Idukki region of Kerala. 2019 the company has started the procurement and primary processing of wet cocoa beans from its member farmers from the Idukki region. The facility area at Ramapuram, near Pala.
